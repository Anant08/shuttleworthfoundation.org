Mai recently joined the Shuttleworth Foundation in a support services role and facilitates all office functions for the Foundation which includes funding requests and applications for the Fellowship Program.

Mai is an experienced project coordinator having worked in leading international software technology companies.

She has a wide range of experience managing projects in a matrix environment with global reach and diverse geographic, cultural and business needs and over the years has grown to appreciate what open source technology can do to empower people, organizations and entire communities.

Mai holds a National Diploma and Teachers Licentiate in Speech and Drama and is also certified yoga teacher. She loves life, animals, art, architecture, hiking, music and collecting vinyl.
