Jaisen Mathai
trovebox.com - Preservation of digital archives

Jaisen Mathai is the founder of Trovebox and working on software which abstracts underlying storage systems into a unified API. Trovebox includes web and mobile apps to create, organize, collaborate and share digital media. Supported storage systems include popular services such as Dropbox and Amazon S3 as well as obscure services like Archive.org and proprietary internal systems. Jaisen will consider his work a success once his great grandchildren are able to see photos, videos and other media of their great grand parents.
