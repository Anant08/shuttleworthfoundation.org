Daniel Lombraña González
CrowdCrafting.org - Open Citizen Science

Daniel Lombraña González is an open-source developer and a researcher with a passion for citizen science. Daniel is building an open source web-based framework for citizen science projects called PyBossa, where researchers and citizens can create their own scientific applications. A good example of the framework is CrowdCrafting where volunteers can help in antimatter projects, geo-locate mosquitoes traps for malaria control, etc.
